Our Ales

Timothy Taylor Landlord: 4.3% ABV, West Yorkshire - A classic, pale ale with complex citrus and hoppy aromas. The Meryl Streep of beers, its many awards include Camra’s ‘Champion beer of Britain’. Best enjoyed with our home-made, beer battered fish and chips!

Sharp's Doom Bar: 4% ABV, Cornwall - A juicy and refreshing amber ale with a great bitter-sweet balance. The UK's number one selling cask beer. The perfect partner to one of Mr Barrick's, hot water pastry, pork pies (available every evening).

Dark Star Hophead: 3.8%, Brighton - A clean drinking, pale, golden ale with a strong floral aroma and notes of elderflower. A great session beer - knock it back with our super tasty, classic beef burger. A match made in heaven!

Black Sheep Best Bitter: 3.8%, North Yorkshire - A light, clean drinking and moreish amber ale with plenty of peppery hop and a long bitter finish. A proper Yorkshire beer to be enjoyed alongside proper pub food - try with our Pie of the day.
